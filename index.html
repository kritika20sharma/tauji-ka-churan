Their Story

A Life Lived in Service of Health

01

A Mother Who Lived 108 Years

It began with a mother who lived to 108 years. Sumer Chand ji — everyone calls him Tauji — watched his mother live a long, healthy, radiant life on simple Ayurvedic remedies. He took note of every herb, every proportion, every season. He understood early that nature, when respected, gives generously.

02

Conversations in Village Buses

Tauji didn't advertise. He talked to people — in buses, in village gatherings, at local haats. With honest words and a warm smile, he shared what worked. People came back. They brought their families. Word spread through the hills of Himachal Pradesh, slowly and surely, the way truth always does.

03

Sarita Devi — The Heart of Every Batch

Behind every packet is Sarita Devi — Tauji's partner in everything. She sorts the herbs by hand, grinds them slowly, and mixes each batch with patience and care. No machines. No shortcuts. Just two people who believe that what you put in your body must be made with love and intention.

04

Small Batches by Design

From a small home in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, this is not a business. This is a calling. Every batch is small because they believe large quantity means lost quality. When they sell out, they wait — until the next harvest is right, the weather is right, and the herbs are at their best.

" We have not changed our recipes in 40 years. Why would we? They still work. — Sumer Chand (Tauji)
